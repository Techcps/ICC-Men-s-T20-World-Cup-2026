# 🏏 **ICC Men's T20 World Cup 2026 – Best Playing XI**

A Python-powered analysis that selects the ultimate Best XI from the ICC Men's T20 World Cup 2026 using real match performance data.

## **Key features:**
- Categorizes players by role: Batter, Wicketkeeper, All-rounder, Bowler
- Computes a custom performance score (weighted combination of runs scored, wickets taken, strike rate, economy, etc.)
- Ranks and selects top performers per role
- Visualizes results with clean Matplotlib bar charts, color-coded by team

Built with: **Python · Pandas · Matplotlib**

***Goal**: Eliminate selection bias and let pure stats decide the dream team — no opinions, just numbers.

Perfect for cricket analytics enthusiasts, and data lovers!
